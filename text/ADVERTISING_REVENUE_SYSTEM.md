### **3. Revenue Models**

#### **CPM (Cost Per Mille) - Impressions**
- **Minimum**: ‚Ç¶50 per 1,000 impressions
- **Revenue**: Platform earns percentage based on placement
- **Example**: 10,000 impressions √ó ‚Ç¶100 CPM = ‚Ç¶1,000 ‚Üí Platform earns ‚Ç¶600-800

#### **CPC (Cost Per Click)**
- **Minimum**: ‚Ç¶5 per click
- **Revenue**: Platform earns percentage of click costs
- **Example**: 100 clicks √ó ‚Ç¶20 CPC = ‚Ç¶2,000 ‚Üí Platform earns ‚Ç¶1,200-1,600

#### **CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)**
- **Revenue**: Platform earns from conversion-based campaigns
- **Example**: 10 conversions √ó ‚Ç¶500 CPA = ‚Ç¶5,000 ‚Üí Platform earns ‚Ç¶3,250-4,000
